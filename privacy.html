Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Overview

Pirimid ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at pirimid.ai and use our interactive tools, including the Welding Productivity ROI Calculator.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address — when you contact us via info@pirimid.ai
  • Meeting booking details — when you schedule a meeting through our embedded Outlook booking widget

Information collected automatically:

  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time of visit) — used for analytics and security
  • Usage analytics — aggregated, anonymized data on how visitors interact with our site and demo tools

3. ROI Calculator Inputs

The Welding Productivity ROI Calculator processes all inputs entirely in your browser. No data you enter into the calculator (welder count, hourly rates, productivity improvements, etc.) is transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere. All calculations run client-side using JavaScript.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To schedule and manage meeting requests
  • To maintain website security and analyze traffic patterns
  • To improve our website and services

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Fonts — to load the Inter font family. Google may collect standard request data (IP address, user agent).
  • Chart.js CDN — loaded from jsDelivr for the interactive demo chart.
  • Microsoft Outlook Bookings — for scheduling meetings. Their privacy policy applies to data entered in the booking form.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@pirimid.ai.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@pirimid.ai.
